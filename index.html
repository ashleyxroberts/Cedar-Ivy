About

Jigsaw Therapies is a counselling and wellbeing service providing resources and training in counselling skills.

TRAINING

Introduction to Counselling Skills

This course teaches you the basics of counselling in an experiential way within an atmosphere of support and challenge. You will be introduced to the three main aspects of counselling training: personal development, listening and focusing skills, and theoretical comprehension.
The fundamental theory will be illustrated through interactive group and one to one practice counselling sessions. This work will be supplimented with additional reading material, as well as, a reflective journal exercise to inform theory and examine your own motivations. As a result you will have a knowledge of the main approaches behind counselling: psychodynamic, person-centred, transactional analysis, cognitive-behavioural and the core conditions. From this learning, you can prepare for qualified counselling training and decide how you want to move forward toward your goals.
